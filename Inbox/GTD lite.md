And my personal favorite, the one my counselor told me about: (1) Make a master list of all your goals, tasks, etc.. (2) In the morning, make a daily to do list with (A) the stuff that absolutely must get done today and (B) 3 small tasks you gather from your master list. Put away your notebook and just look at the daily to do list.
